Today's Main Street

With almost 70% of the worlds population online, the internet has become todays "Main Street". Businesses all over the world now have access to virtually all countries, all while not establishing a physical storefront. By creating a website to ones business, clients can easily access it by typing a website adress. People can now shop online 24/7 or run their own small business with easier access due to cheaper, sometimes free websites (etsy, shopify, depop, etc.) It is crucial for large business to have an online presence in order to reach clients from all over thr world.
